Cassandra Mobley | UX/UI Designer

I'm a multi-disciplined individual specializing in front-end development with a background in Engineering. My whole world is centered around my love for product design in digital and physical form. This passion allows me to delve deep into each process of the design to solve problems. Even though I've had a focus educational wise in manufacturing I've always dreamed of designing and integrating products.
This has lead me to develop a multi-technology based platform that teaches kids financial literacy through gamification with a line of books that tie into the topics for a younger audience. This curiousness has also lead to learning basic welding to create physical storage solutions and other prototypes in board games.
I've specialized in Solidworks, Autocad, and a array of Adobe Creative Cloud applications. I also have a background coding in C++, HTML, CSS, and have worked on designs in Unity.

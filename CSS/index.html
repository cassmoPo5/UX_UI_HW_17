About

Cassandra Mobley | UX/UI Designer

With a specialization in front-end development and a background in Industrial Engineering, Florida born Cassandra Mobley has a love for product design in digital and physical form. This allows her to delve deep into each process of the design to solve problems. Even though she's had a focus educational wise in manufacturing she's always dreamed of designing and integrating products. Even going as far as learning basic welding to create physical storage solutions or learning code from online classes to gamify financial learning. She is proficient in Solidworks, Autocad, and a array of Adobe Creative Cloud applications. She also has a background coding in C++, HTML, CSS, and has worked on designs in Unity due to personal projects from her own life.
